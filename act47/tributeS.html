"To my Siblings"
Jerelle & Jona Aleonar

My siblings always made me feel included, sure, we all annoyed each other at times but when one was feeling vulnerable the other would always do their best to helps out. Through thick and thin, they've always shown me their strengths, and trusted me enough to share their weaknesses. They always supported me unless they believed I was in the wrong, and they always made good points as to why, always pointing me to the right direction.
