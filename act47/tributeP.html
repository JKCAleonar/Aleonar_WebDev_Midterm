"To my Parents"
Alona & Jonas Aleonar

My parents have always taught me the basics of how to live and not just survive. To enjoy my life and not let small things weigh me down, they're strong, passionate and also worrywarts at points. They always make sure I take care of myself before taking care of other things. Just as they have a strong side they also have a sensitive and vulnerable side, making heart to heart talks intense. I am always grateful for their help, resources and effort.
