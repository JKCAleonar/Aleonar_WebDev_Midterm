Breathe

Atlas has a weight to carry & so do I
Pressure & Gravity making me comply

Considered a blessing as well as a curse
Since when did doing my best make me feel worse

A simple task, I overcomplicate
Thoughts going too fast for my hands to create

Praise is appreciated every now and then
Until people expect the best again and again

A bit suffocating I must admit
But I endure, apologies to my health, as I always risk it

I can't turn back now, I've come too far
Pushing out the best I can manage, never settling for subpar

Unfinished work, files untouched, this alone I cannot leave
So in the midst of this, all I can do,
is Breathe.
